<p><strong>The Melamine Molding Compound Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Compression Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Melamine Molding Compound market is primarily segmented into two types: injection molding and compression molding. Injection molding involves injecting the melamine compound into a mold, allowing for complex shapes and high precision in production. This method is efficient for large-scale manufacturing. In contrast, compression molding involves placing the melamine compound into a heated mold, where it is then compressed to form the desired shape. This technique is often used for thicker parts and can accommodate larger production volumes, though it may be slower than injection molding.</p></p>
