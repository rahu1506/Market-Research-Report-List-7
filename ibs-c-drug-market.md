<p><p>The IBS-C drug market comprises various treatment types aimed at alleviating symptoms of Irritable Bowel Syndrome with constipation. Amitiza (Lubiprostone) enhances fluid secretion in the intestines, promoting bowel movements. Lactulose is an osmotic laxative that softens stools by drawing water into the colon. Milk of Magnesia, another osmotic agent, provides relief by easing stool passage. LINZESS (Linaclotide) works by increasing intestinal fluid secretion and transit. Other medications include different laxatives and dietary supplements, addressing diverse patient needs.</p></p>
