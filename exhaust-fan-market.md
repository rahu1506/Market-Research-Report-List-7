<p><p>The exhaust fan market comprises several types, including centrifugal fans, axial fans, and others. Centrifugal fans utilize a rotating impeller to draw in air and expel it at right angles, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan's axis, providing efficient ventilation for larger spaces. Other types encompass specialized designs, like mixed-flow fans and roof-mounted fans, catering to specific industrial or residential needs, each serving unique airflow and pressure requirements.</p></p>
