<p><p>The Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) equipment market encompasses various techniques used to evaluate materials without causing damage. Eddy Current Testing employs electromagnetic induction to detect surface and subsurface flaws in conductive materials. Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing utilizes magnetic fields to identify defects in ferromagnetic materials, often used in pipeline inspections. Ultrasonic Testing employs high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws and measure material thickness. Other methods may include radiographic and visual inspections, expanding the capabilities and applications of NDT technologies.</p></p>
