<p><p>The deployment of Virtual Packet Core (VPC) can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based deployment leverages virtualized resources hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, which are ideal for operators looking to reduce infrastructure investment. Conversely, on-premise deployment involves installing the VPC directly on the operator's infrastructure, providing greater control and security. Each option caters to different operational needs and preferences, influencing the choice based on specific business requirements.</p></p>
