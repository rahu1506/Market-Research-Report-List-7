<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust</li><li>Outside Exhaust</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental air turbine market is segmented into two primary types: inside exhaust and outside exhaust. Inside exhaust turbines direct air and exhaust internally, often leading to quieter operation and reduced contamination risk, making them suitable for sterile environments. Outside exhaust turbines expel air and debris externally, which can enhance visibility during procedures but may be noisier. Both types cater to different practitioner preferences and operational needs, impacting overall efficiency and clinical outcomes in dental practices.</p></p>
