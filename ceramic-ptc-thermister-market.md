<p><p>The Ceramic PTC Thermistor market consists of two primary types: Surface Mount Type and Radial Leaded Type. </p><p>Surface Mount Type thermistors are designed for compact electronic applications, allowing easy integration into printed circuit boards (PCBs) due to their minimal footprint. </p><p>Radial Leaded Type thermistors feature wire leads that extend radially from a central body, making them suitable for through-hole mounting in a variety of applications, particularly where space is less constrained. Both types effectively provide temperature sensing and overcurrent protection.</p></p>
