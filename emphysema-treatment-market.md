<p><p>The Emphysema Treatment Market is categorized into Centrilobular and Panlobular emphysema types. Centrilobular emphysema mainly affects the central parts of the lungs and is commonly associated with smoking, leading to specific treatment strategies focused on symptom management and improving lung function. Panlobular emphysema, on the other hand, involves uniform destruction of the lung's air sacs and is often linked to genetic factors like alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency, requiring targeted therapies that may include advanced interventions and personalized care approaches.</p></p>
