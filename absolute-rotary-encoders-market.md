<p><p>The absolute rotary encoders market is categorized into multi-turn and single-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders provide a unique position within one rotation, offering high precision for applications where the rotation range is limited. Multi-turn encoders, on the other hand, track multiple rotations, enabling accurate position measurement over several turns. This capability is ideal for applications requiring extensive positional feedback, such as robotics and industrial automation. Both types serve distinct needs, driving innovations across various sectors.</p></p>
