<p><p>The electrostatic sensor market is divided into two main types: hand-held and in-line sensors. Hand-held sensors are portable devices used for spot-checking electrostatic charges in various environments, allowing users to quickly and conveniently assess static levels. In-line sensors, on the other hand, are integrated into production processes to continuously monitor electrostatic discharge during operations. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as electronics and manufacturing, helping maintain quality and prevent damage from static electricity.</p></p>
