<p><p>The Industrial Metal Additive Manufacturing (AM) Printer market encompasses various technologies, including Powder Bed Fusion (PBF), Material Extrusion (ME), and Directed Energy Deposition (DED). PBF uses a laser or electron beam to fuse powdered metal layer by layer. Material Extrusion involves extruding heated metal filaments to build parts. DED adds material through a focused energy source, typically for repairs or geometric enhancements. Other methods may include binder jetting or hybrid systems, catering to diverse industrial applications and requirements.</p></p>
