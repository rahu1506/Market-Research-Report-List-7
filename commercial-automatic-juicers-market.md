<p><p>The commercial automatic juicer market encompasses three primary types: centrifugal juicers, masticating juicers, and others. Centrifugal juicers use high-speed spinning to extract juice quickly, ideal for soft fruits and vegetables. Masticating juicers operate at slower speeds, thoroughly crushing and squeezing ingredients, resulting in higher juice yield and nutrient retention, suitable for leafy greens. The "others" category includes specialized juicers like twin-gear or hydraulic models, catering to niche markets with specific juicing needs or higher production capabilities.</p></p>
