<p><strong>The Smart Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Cards</li><li>Contactless Cards</li><li>Memory Cards</li><li>CPU/MPU Microprocessor Multifunction Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart card market encompasses several types, each serving distinct functions. Contact cards require physical insertion into a reader, commonly used in banking. Contactless cards utilize RFID technology, allowing transactions without direct contact, ideal for public transport and quick payments. Memory cards store data but have limited processing capabilities, primarily used in applications like loyalty programs. CPU/MPU microprocessor multifunction cards integrate advanced processing power, enabling secure transactions, biometrics, and multiple applications, making them versatile for various industries.</p></p>
