<p><p>The Large Flow Ultrapure Water System market primarily comprises two key types: Reverse Osmosis + Ion Exchange and Reverse Osmosis + EDI (Electrodeionization). The Reverse Osmosis + Ion Exchange system utilizes a combination of membrane filtration and ion exchange resin to remove impurities, providing high-purity water suitable for various industrial applications. Conversely, the Reverse Osmosis + EDI system enhances water purification by using electrochemical processes to further reduce ion content, ensuring even higher ultrapure water quality essential for industries like pharmaceuticals and electronics.</p></p>
