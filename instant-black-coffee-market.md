<p><ul><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Freeze Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Instant Black Coffee market consists of two main production methods: spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves rapidly transforming liquid coffee extract into a powder by passing it through hot air, resulting in a quicker production process. In contrast, freeze drying preserves the coffee's flavor and aroma by freezing the extract and then removing moisture under vacuum conditions. Both methods cater to consumer demand for convenience and quality in instant coffee products, influencing market dynamics and preferences.</p></p>
