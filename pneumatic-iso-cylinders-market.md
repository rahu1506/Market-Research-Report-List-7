<p><p>The pneumatic ISO cylinders market consists of three primary types: single-acting cylinders, double-acting cylinders, and others. Single-acting cylinders use air pressure to move in one direction and rely on a spring or gravity for return, making them simpler and cost-effective. Double-acting cylinders utilize air pressure for both extending and retracting actions, offering greater control and force. "Others" encompasses specialized or unique cylinder designs that cater to specific applications or industries, thus expanding market diversity.</p></p>
