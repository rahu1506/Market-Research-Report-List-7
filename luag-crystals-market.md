<p><ul><li>LuAG(Ce) Crystals</li><li>LuAG(Pr) Crystals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LuAG crystals are classified into LuAG(Ce) and LuAG(Pr) based on their doping elements. LuAG(Ce) crystals, doped with cerium, are widely used in scintillation detectors and solid-state lighting due to their excellent luminescent properties. In contrast, LuAG(Pr) crystals, doped with praseodymium, are primarily utilized in laser applications because of their unique optical characteristics. Both types serve distinct roles in high-tech industries, contributing to advancements in medical imaging, telecommunications, and radiation detection technologies.</p></p>
