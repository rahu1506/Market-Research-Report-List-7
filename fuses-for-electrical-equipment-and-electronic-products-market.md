<p><p>Fuses for electrical equipment and electronic products serve to protect circuits from overloads and faults. The market features several types: </p><p>1. **Filamentous Fuses** utilize a thin wire that melts when excessive current flows.</p><p>2. **Flake Fuses** are designed with a thin, flat element providing rapid response to overcurrents.</p><p>3. **Glass Tube Fuses** encapsulate the filament in glass, offering visibility for inspection.</p><p>4. **Ceramic Tube Fuses** use robust ceramic for better thermal resistance and durability, ideal for heavier applications. </p><p>Each type caters to specific applications and requirements in circuit protection.</p></p>
