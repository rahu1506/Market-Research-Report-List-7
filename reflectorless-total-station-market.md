<p><p>A Reflectorless Total Station is a modern surveying instrument that combines the functionalities of an electronic distance measuring device and an angle measuring device, allowing land surveyors to measure distances without the need for a physical reflective target. The growing demand for precise surveying due to urban development, infrastructure projects, and land management is driving the market for reflectorless total stations.</p><p>Market growth is influenced by advancements in technology, including improvements in data processing capabilities and integration with software solutions for mapping and modeling.
