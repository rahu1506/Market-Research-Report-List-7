<p><p>The diaphragm for vanadium cell market consists of three main types: cation exchange membranes, anion exchange membranes, and amphoteric ion exchange membranes. Cation exchange membranes selectively allow positive ions to pass, enhancing the efficiency of vanadium redox flow batteries. Anion exchange membranes facilitate the movement of negative ions, contributing to overall ion balance. Amphoteric ion exchange membranes can transport both cations and anions, offering versatile applications. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing performance and energy storage in vanadium cell systems.</p></p>
