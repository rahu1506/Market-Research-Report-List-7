<p><p>The Emergency Lighting System market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Self-Contained Power Systems** independently house batteries and lights, ensuring functionality during power outages without central support. </p><p>2. **Central Power Systems** utilize a centralized battery to power multiple fixtures, offering a robust, efficient solution for larger facilities and lowering maintenance costs. </p><p>3. **Hybrid Power Systems** combine both self-contained and central systems, providing flexibility and reliability by balancing individual unit autonomy with centralized control for optimal emergency lighting performance.</p></p>
