<p><p>Plate heat exchangers are categorized into several types based on their construction and application. Brazed heat exchangers consist of stainless steel plates bonded together with a brazing material, offering compactness and high efficiency. Gasketed plate and frame units utilize elastomer gaskets for sealing, enabling easy maintenance and versatility. Welded plate exchangers feature plates that are welded together, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Additional categories may include semi-welded and spiral heat exchangers, catering to specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
