<p><ul><li>Main-Stream Capnographs</li><li>Side-Stream Capnographs</li><li>Micro-Stream Capnographs</li><li>Capnography Disposables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capnography market includes various types catering to different clinical needs. Main-stream capnographs provide real-time CO2 monitoring directly from the airway, offering quick response times. Side-stream capnographs collect gas samples through a tube, allowing continuous monitoring with less impact on airflow. Micro-stream capnographs use a lower volume of gas for analysis, enhancing portability and efficiency. Additionally, the capnography disposables market encompasses single-use items like sampling lines and filters, ensuring hygiene and accuracy in medical settings.</p></p>
